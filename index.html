Our Story

Where Every Story Begins

Initium — Latin for "beginning." We chose this name because we believe great things start here: first dates, anniversary dinners, study sessions that turn into friendships, and post-campus celebrations that become memories for life.

Born in the heart of Hinjawadi, Pune's most vibrant tech and student hub, Initium Cafe & More was founded with one obsession: to create a space where luxury doesn't come with a premium price tag. Where the food is authentic, the ambiance is cinematic, and every visit feels genuinely special.

We started small — a vision, a kitchen, and a belief that Pune deserved something different. No shortcuts on ingredients. No compromises on experience. Real flavours, real warmth, real care.

Authenticity

Every recipe is original. No shortcuts, no box ingredients — just honest, crafted food.

Ambiance First

We design every corner for romance, calm, and that "this is exactly what I needed" feeling.

Community Rooted

Built for Hinjawadi's students, tech professionals, couples and families alike.

Worth Every Rupee

Luxury that's budget-friendly. Premium experience, accessible pricing — always.

Our Vision for Tomorrow

We're growing — but slowly, intentionally. Initium plans to expand its chef's table experience, introduce weekend live acoustic sessions, and launch curated tasting events for food lovers across Pune. The dream? To be the place every Punekar points to when someone asks: "Where should we go for something truly special?"
