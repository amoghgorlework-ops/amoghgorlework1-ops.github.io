Our Story

Born from a
Bold Idea

"Pune deserved a space where luxury and authenticity could coexist — where a student and a newlywed couple could both feel right at home."

Initium was born from a simple but radical belief: great food and beautiful spaces should not be the exclusive privilege of the few. We opened our doors in Hinjawadi with a singular mission — to create Pune's most honest luxury experience.

The name itself is a statement. Initium — Latin for "beginning" — because every great story, every meaningful relationship, every memorable meal starts somewhere. We want to be that somewhere for you.

Today, Initium stands as one of Hinjawadi's most beloved destinations, with a 4.9-star rating built not on marketing, but on genuine love from guests who return again and again. We are just getting started.

The future: A second location in Baner, a weekend brunch series, and a chef's table experience for intimate parties of six.
